About Us

01

Cygna Space Society is the space, astronomy and innovation hub of Amity International School, Sec-46, gurugram. It provides a platform for the students to nurture their skills not only in space science, but also gain mastery in Creativity, Problem Solving, Management skills, etc. as well, that are crucial in today’s school and work environments. Competing in competitions all about space, astronomy and engineering like the Space Settlement Design, the National Space Society’s Space Settlement Contest, prestigious competitions on the international stage. Also including various other competitions on different levels, wherein our members have repeatedly won accolades. We strive to provide the conditions to nurture skills in one of the most up and coming industries of Space Sciences and Engineering. Providing an experience grounded in the real world, for the future real world
